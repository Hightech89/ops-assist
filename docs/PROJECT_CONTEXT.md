# Project Context

Ops Assist is an AI-powered manufacturing troubleshooting assistant.

The idea originated from real manufacturing floor experience where troubleshooting systems are often static, difficult to search, and unable to interpret unusual symptom combinations.

This project is being developed for the DEV.to Gemma 4 Challenge.

## Primary Goal

Use Gemma 4 to provide contextual troubleshooting assistance for factory operators and maintenance teams.

## Intended Users

- Machine operators
- Manufacturing technicians
- Maintenance personnel
- New hires in industrial environments

## Core Concept

Users describe:
- machine issues
- alarms
- symptoms
- abnormal behavior

The AI responds with:
- likely causes
- troubleshooting steps
- safety guidance
- escalation recommendations

## Development Philosophy

- Keep the MVP small and polished
- Prioritize usability over complexity
- Avoid overengineering
- Focus on realistic industrial workflows
